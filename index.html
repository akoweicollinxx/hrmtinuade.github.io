HRM Tinuade Babalola Adejuyigbe

Welcome to the official website of HRM AMB Tinuade Babalola Adejuyigbe, the first female monarch and the Adele1(crowned Princess) of Iboropa Akoko kingdom of Ondo State.

Projects

HRM Tinuade Babalola Adejuyigbe is a business woman, philanthropist and a lover of girl child empowerment which was the reason she unveiled her foundation during her 23rd birthday party in 2021 which is targeted at empowering women and children especially in the agricultural sector.

About

Adele1

HRM Tinuade Babalola Adejuyigbe Adele1 (crowned Princess) ORO VII of Iboropa Akoko Ondo State is born to HRM Oba Emmanuel Babalola Adejuyigbe and his wife Olori Olusola Aderibigbe Adejuyigbe on may 25th 1998, she was crowned Regent of Iboropa Akoko at the age of 16 in Febuary 23rd 2015 after the death of her father to rule the kingdom pending when a new king gets crowned and she handed over the throne on 16th december 2021 to HRM Oba Babatunde Olaleye who is the current Oluboropa of Iboropa Akoko kingdom.

A graduate from Adekunle Ajasin University Akungba Akoko Ondo State with a degree in Public Administration in 2019, she is the most celebrated Regent in Nigeria due to her many accomplishments at a very young age.
